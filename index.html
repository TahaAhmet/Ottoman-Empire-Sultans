The Ottoman Empire's Sultans

Osman I (Gazi)
Osman I

Reign: 1299–1324

Founder of the Ottoman Empire. Established the state in Söğüt.

Orhan Gazi
Orhan

Reign: 1324–1362

Conquered Bursa.

Murad I
Murad I

Reign: 1362–1389

Conquered Edirne. Martyred during the Battle of Kosovo.

Bayezid I
Bayezid I

Reign: 1389–1402

Nicknamed "The Thunderbolt". Defeated by Timur in Battle of Ankara.

Mehmed I
Mehmed I

Reign: 1413–1421

Reunited the empire after the Interregnum. Called the second founder.

Murad II
Murad II

Reign: 1421–1444 and 1446-1451

Secured Balkans with Varna victory. Father of Mehmed the Conqueror.

Mehmed II
Mehmed II

Reign: 1451–1481

Conquered Constantinople (Istanbul) in 1453, ending Byzantine Empire.

Bayezid II
Bayezid II

Reign: 1481–1512

Known as "The Just". Welcomed refugees from Spain. Abdicated later.

Selim I
Selim I

Reign: 1512–1520

Conquered Egypt and Syria. Became the first Ottoman Caliph.

Suleiman I
Suleiman I

Reign: 1520–1566

"The Magnificent". Peak of Ottoman power, culture, and law.

Selim II
Selim II

Reign: 1566–1574

Conquered Cyprus. Selim II is generally regarded as the first Ottoman sultan who did not personally lead the army into campaign.

Ahmed I
Ahmed I

Reign: 1603–1617

Built the Blue Mosque. Changed succession system to seniority.

Osman II
Osman II

Reign: 1618–1622

Young Osman. Tried to abolish Janissaries but was killed by them.

Murad IV
Murad IV

Reign: 1623–1640

Recaptured Baghdad. Known for strict authority and restoring order.

Mehmed IV
Mehmed IV

Reign: 1648–1687

"The Hunter". Largest territorial extent but lost at Vienna (1683).

Ahmed III
Ahmed III

Reign: 1703–1730

Tulip Period sultan. Era of peace, arts, and first printing press.

Mahmud I
Mahmud I

Reign: 1730–1754

Started military reforms. Successful against Austria and Russia.

Abdülhamid I
Abdülhamid I

Reign: 1774–1789

Lost Crimea (Küçük Kaynarca). Known for his benevolence.

Selim III
Selim III

Reign: 1789–1807

Formed Nizam-i Cedid army. Musician and reformer. Deposed.

Mahmud II
Mahmud II

Reign: 1808–1839

Abolished Janissaries (1826). Modernized clothing and state.

Abdülmecid I
Abdülmecid I

Reign: 1839–1861

Started Tanzimat reforms. Built Dolmabahçe Palace.

Abdülaziz
Abdülaziz

Reign: 1861–1876

First sultan to visit Western Europe. Expanded the Navy.

Abdülhamid II
Abdülhamid II

Reign: 1876–1909

Promulgated first constitution. Emphasized Pan-Islamism.

Mehmed V
Mehmed V

Reign: 1909–1918

Figurehead during WWI and Young Turk regime rule.

Mehmed VI
Mehmed VI

Reign: 1918–1922

Last Sultan. Left Istanbul after sultanate was abolished.

This website is not include all of the Ottoman Empire Sultans.
